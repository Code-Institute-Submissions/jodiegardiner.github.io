It is a dark time for the students. The Institute, led by their relentless taskmaster Emperor Juanmatine and his iron right hand, Darth Darragh - Lord of the Git, have demanded that all must submit a project demonstrating their competence with front-end technologies.

But there is a glimmer of hope for the embattled students. A pair of mystical powers known as "boot-strap" and "jQuery" have recently emerged. With careful training, perhaps the students will be able to bend these forces to their will...
